To group documents and applications with functional criteria
Objectives of this application > To group documents and applications with functional criteria

In many tasks (eg, for a legal claim, or during the writing of a book, or to create a doctoral thesis, etc.) it is very productive to have a common point to access all the documents related to that work, regardless of their physical locations.

The Desktop Organizer uses the concepts of "Project" (alias "configuration file"), and "Group" for this functionality.

The Group concept can also be thought as "reading list". Another example can be found in "Photo album".
