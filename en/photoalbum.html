Photo album
Use cases > Photo album

Photos are documents that lend themselves to many forms of classification, since:

  • They have an associated date.
  • May refer to one or more people, humans groups, companies,
  • May refer to a particular place.
  • May refer to a trip.
  • We may associate it with our personal comments.
  • We may want to relate them to other documents or photos.

The Desktop Organizer can help in the creation configuration files that ease the classification of photos, adding simple to use searching mechanism to locate those that meet certain criteria.

In this case, the key concept is "Tag". For example:

  • We may create a Grupo for each "Viewing list"  associated to concepts (set of people, trips, etc.)
  • Inside each Group we may create Options that are significant for the concept of the Group.
  • For each one of these Options (photo):
    • Associate tags for the Date of the photo (in the form "a2017","mApril","d12"), and/or the location it was taken.
    • Associate tags for each person/company/etc. present in the photo.
    • Add a comment that include references (hyperlinks) to other documents/photos/URLs, and/or texts that can used for searching.
