Añadir Grupo nuevo

Se puede añadir un Grupo de varias formas:

  1. Through the "Add Group" menu option. See below.
  2. Using the Combined editor for Groups and Options.
  3. As the result of the dragging & drop for the creation of an Option. See here.

Creation of a new Group through the "Add Group" menu.

The "Add Group" menu is shown pressing the mouse right button on the left panel outside any existing Group. See next figure.

Pressing (left button) on this (single option) menu, the following form will appear:

Pressing "Cancel" button will abort the creation of the new Group. Pressing the "Continue" button, the form as in the next figure will appear: you may enter the (initial) "Comment" associated to the newly created Group. It can be modified later.

Group details editing through options in the "Group menu"

Pressing the mouse right button while on a "Group" presents the "Group" menu, as shown in the next figure.

With the menu option "Change Commentary" the application prompts for changing the "Comment" property of the Group. See the possibilities in Editing Comments.

With the menu option "Change Title" the application prompts for changing the Group "Title", presenting the form indicated above.

Deleting a Group

This menu option effectively remove the full content of Group, including all its Options.
