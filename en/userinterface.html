Description of the User Interface

The next figure shows the main form and its components:

  1. The form caption shows the name of the "configuration file" that is being used.
  2. The main menu has two entries:
    1. Desktop Organizer files, shown below, with options explained below.

    1. Help, shown below, that provides information about the configuration file, and two helps: one local and another through Internet.

  1. The body of the main form is divided vertically in two parts:
    1. In the upper left is the Presentation selection box, which allows the selection of the View.

    1. On the left side below is a panel, whose content changes depending on the active View. In the Standard View the available Options are presented, grouped into Groups.
    2. On the right side is the Comment panel: it shows the comment associated with the Option or the Group indicated by the mouse in the left panel. See examples in the following figures.

    1. On the right side below is the text box where the Tags associated with the Option in the left panel on where the mouse is located
